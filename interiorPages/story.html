Our Restaurant

At Végétalienne, we pride ourselves on being a kitchen that specializes in dishes from the plant kingdom, particularly those inspired by vegan French cuisine. The word végétalienne means “vegan” in French.

We embrace a nose-to-tail attitude, ensuring that every peel from a fruit or vegetable becomes a component of a dish and does not go to waste. Every beverage served is derived from either vegan-friendly French drinks or Eastern beverages.

Végétalienne’s ingredients are sourced from local farmers who prioritize organic and high-quality food provided by nature. The restaurant is located in the heart of downtown Houston.
